Of late, while doing research I've often ended up reading about notable people in history who do art on the side. I love reading about a psychiatrist who mainly publish books about psychology, but who's also published a book featuring his own bird cartoons(Robert Jay Lifton). I am so delighted to see this that I've started to collect their names and to keep them here, in this ever-expanding list.

In the words of Dani Rojas, were he an artist instead of a footballer...

"Art is life."

  • Friedrich Engles
    Philosopher, journalist. Sketch and cartoon artist.
  • Robert Jay lifton
    Psychiatrist, writer. Draws humorous cartoons involving birds.
  • Robert Baden-Powell
    British Army Officer, founder of the Boy Scouts Association. Could draw with both hands, liked to make caricatures of people attending meetings he attended, often drew for his own publications.
  • Nikolai Bukharin
    Russian revolutionary, politician and theorist. A caricaturist, would draw cartoons of contemporary Soviet politicians.
