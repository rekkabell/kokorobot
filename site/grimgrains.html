Grim Grains is a lean, and illustrated food blog, it features plant-based recipes and an extensive database of information on a variety of ingredients. It first started in September 2014.

Visit Grim Grains

In 2021, GrimGrains had 70 recipes. In the start the blog was about odd-looking food, I would experiment with color, and shape to make something beautiful. In 2016, I decided that I needed something more practical, and opted for recipes with simpler ingredients. Many of the old recipes are now gone, and live only through images.

In 2016, a photo of my black bread was featured in Inspiration book no28 by MaisonEtObjet.
