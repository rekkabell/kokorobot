  • Name: Nemerald Kozielec
  • Nickname: Nemo, Kozi
  • Alignment: True neutral
  • Gender: Cis male
  • Orientation: Demisexual
  • Appearance: Nemerald is grey haired and purple-eyed. He is of short stature, not especially strong, but has a thick build.

Nemerald is a human physician, and known supporter of the Hegata community. He has an adopted Hegata daughter named Lilli.

Not much is known of his past. Benevolent, he feels the need to teach others, but not all who come to learn return this kindness.

Nemerald is patient and will go through great lengths to help anyone under his care. His clinic in downtown Montore is well-known, he shares the office with another doctor, a Hegata named Jak.
