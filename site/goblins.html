In the words of its creator, Goblin Week is an event in which you make goblins every day for a week, whatever that means to you.. It's a low-pressure community art project, meant to spread joy and creativity, participants are encouraged to "follow their heart." There are no strict rules.

Entries are usually tagged under #goblinweek.
