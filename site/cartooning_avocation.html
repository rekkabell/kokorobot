An ever-expanding list of people whose main occupation isn't art-related, but do cartooning on the side.

  • Friedrich Engles
    Philosopher, journalist. Sketch and cartoon artist.
  • Robert Jay lifton
    Psychiatrist, writer. Draws humorous cartoons involving birds.
  • Robert Baden-Powell
    British Army Officer, founder of the Boy Scouts Association. Could draw with both hands, liked to make caricatures of people attending meetings he attended, often drew for his own publications.
  • Nikolai Bukharin
    Russian revolutionary, politician and theorist. A caricaturist, would draw cartoons of contemporary Soviet politicians.
  • William Makepeace Thackeray
    Writer.
  • Flannery O’Connor
    Writer. She often said that she could draw better than she could write. She was an avid cartoonist and painter.
  • John Updike
    Novelist, art and literary critic.
  • Guy Davenport
    Writer, teacher, translator. Had a little-talked-about life as a painter and cartoonist. He drew satirical illustrations for literary critic publications, he also enjoyed producing illustrations to accompany hisown stories.
