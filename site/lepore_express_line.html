Take a seat aboard the Lepore Express, listen to a bit of music, talk to some people, play games, look outside, kickback.

Lepore Express Line is devine and I's humble submission to Catjam 2026, the game features a ton of cameos from all our other projects, maybe you can recognize a few. It's written in Uxntal, a concatenative assembly language for a 16-bit ordinator. Have a look at the source.

Play Lepore Express Line
