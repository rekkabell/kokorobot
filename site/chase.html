Chase

Context. Many human hostiles go to the woods with the goal to hunt Dae(also known as the ToT, the 'Terror of the Taiga'), and the Solomon's Devils down. On one of his runs, he stumbles on a camp. Dae is worried, and decides to go and take a closer look.

The style changes partway through, before I re-did part of the sequence to make it in line with the new storyline. The first part was done in 2021 and was inked by hand and shaded digitally, the more recent one in 2026 and was inked and shaded by hand(pencils and ink).
