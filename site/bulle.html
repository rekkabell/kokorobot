Bulle, Tartine, Momo and other characters of the Mon Ami Bulle app, travel and make discoveries in unique universes. This project was contract work for the IOS app Mon ami Bulle by Télé Québec. See a short video about the project.

I worked on many assets for the animated sequences, and did the artwork for 3 of the main narrated stories of the game. The stories were my main task. The project was published in 2016, I worked on the project in 2015.

It was a really lovely project, made with love by an amazing team, but unfortunately the app was discontinued. I believe the animation segments are still available to watch on TV.

Visit Tele-Quebec for more information.
