2025

This year, instead of a long-form text, I've decided to break down my year into lists. It is possible to see longer form texts of my year anyway through my update log.

Personal achievements

  • Began playing squash.
  • I can do 5 pull-ups, after starting from zero this summer.
  • Learned to take care of my teeth better (see keeping teeth healthy).
  • Deepened my knowledge of sewing, marine diesel engines, stovetop baking, and of basic first-aid.
  • Celebrated Dev & I's 9th year living aboard pino, and our studio hundredrabbits' 10 year anniversary.
  • Sailed to Ucluelet.
  • Been sober for 2 years.

  • Installed 2 new solar panels for Pino, re-coated Teapot with new gelcoat

  • Released 5 new rabbit waves pages:

  • Released Polycat for Catjam 2025.
  • Started punk rabbits, with the goal of drawing 100 rabbits.
