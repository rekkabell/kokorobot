Make a request

Email me at rek ౷ kokorobot · ca to request an original custom drawing. See examples of requested art: sprite, couple and misc commissions.

I am not interested in producing NFts for anyone, nor do I accept crypto payments.

Once in a while, I illustrate and sell original ink drawings. The subjects vary, but often include plants. Below is a list of my current series:

  • fungi
  • knots
  • ginger roots
  • fasciation
  • vancouver island mushrooms(ongoing)

They are drawn on 300 gsm watercolour paper. The size of the drawing are either 152x152mm(6X6 in) or 148x210mm(6x8 in) and the price varies, depending on the complexity of the design. I am only selling original copies (I don't make duplicates), and the price can range from 50-100$ per drawing (+ shipping).
