Nov 7

Devine has a show planned on my birthday. It wasn't their choice, it just happened to be on that date. I wasn't upset when I first learned it months ago, but for some reason today... it bothered me. I think it's because I sometimes feel that our closest friends in the marina don't really care about me, they only care for Devine, so the Devine's show being on my birthday... it felt like they were once again the center of attention. It sucks, because I don't usually care about my birthday. I don't have plans. Maybe I was upset because there suddenly being something happening on my birthday made this all the more evident. I never have plans, I have no friends, etc.

Dec 8

Was going through a bin filled with my sketchbooks, and old souvenirs. I found a project from a class I had in high school, consisting of a book filled with long texts about myself, how I feel etc. I was not a happy person, I was lonely, felt I had no friends, that no one could see me. I felt sad reading that, because I still feel the same way. I also read a letter from my aunt that she wrote me when I graduated high school, it was a lovely, heartfelt letter. I felt sad because my mom told me recently that my aunt didn't talk to anyone in the family, that she was alone a lot, didn't all, or anything... reading that letter, seeing her wording, "she's like me" I thought.
I am innacessible by mom told me. I can feel it, but I don't know what to do about it. I don't know how to appear friendlier, more open, I don't know what to do.c
