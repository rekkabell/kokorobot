A guide I illustrated to accompany the release of Donsol NES, a game written entirely in 6502 assembly (published in 2020). The booklet is available with the purchase of the game. The game was ported to uxn in October 2021.

You are free to print the booklet for your personal use, the box art and cartridge cover are also included in the download. The logo of the cartridge cover was made using Dotgrid.

If you wish to print the box, take note that the size is for Famicom cartridges, measuring 7.0 cm (2.75 inches) in length, and 10.8 cm (4.25 inches) in width. The booklet is designed to fit within this box size.
