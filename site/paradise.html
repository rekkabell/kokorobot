Paradise is an interactive-fiction playground.

In Paradise, you are but a force acting upon vessels, or places, objects and words. In this anonymous world you can create anything, traverse vastly different universes, and share your world with others.

The code the project was done by devine, I drew the various little line illustrations present in the game.
