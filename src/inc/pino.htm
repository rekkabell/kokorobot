  • Size: 10 m
  • Type: Yamaha 33
  • Year: 1982
  • Material: Fibreglass
  • Countries Pino has visited: United States (west coast and Southeast AK), Mexico, French Polynesia, Cook Islands, Tonga, Niue, New Zealand, Fiji, Marshall Islands, and Japan
  • Distance covered: 23,667 nautical miles

Pino has been home to me and my partner {Devine} since 2016. We have traveled around the south and north Pacific together, and experienced many great and difficult things. We have been living aboard since 2016.

My sailboat is a Japanese boat. It was manufactured in Shizuoka and put together in the Pacific Northwest. It is a simple boat, with simple systems. Pino has solar panels, a wood stove for heating, a dry toilet, no refrigeration, no hot water or water pressure, and a minimum of high-power devices.
