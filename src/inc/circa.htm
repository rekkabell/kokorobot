What is Circa

Circa was a comic, drawn by hand and published online.

What to say about Circa? It was a comic project I started in the fall of 2019, but I've only done two chapters and have no intention of continuing. Why? Because I had ideas for a project but I was fuzzy on the details, for me, the best way to flesh out a story is to play it out. Sometimes, playing it out means making a short comic. While making it, I realized that I didn't want to put certain characters in the forefront, I wanted to focus on the Hegata characters (hence why I stopped at chapter 2). I'll leave the comic online, but won't update it anymore. The new project I am making with some of these same characters is called {Hakum}

The characters of Circa have been part of my life for a long, long time now. I've re-used them in a variety of projects, all personal, and this is yet another iteration. I will forever love getting lost in their world. I wrote a short featuring Sy and Token called {Go North}. Also, see some {old art}.
