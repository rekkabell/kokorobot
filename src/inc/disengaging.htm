Disengaging

Context. This is during a time before the Solomon's Devils existed, not too long after Kaddali and Hazaki met at a protest against a development project in the Taiga. "Half-lifers", or "Near-deads", are common insults used by katkum, used towards humans to refer to the fact that they live very short lives.

In the below comic, the front of Hakazi's shirt reads, "Stay out of the Taiga half-lifers", and the back reads, "Save a tree, bury a near dead."
