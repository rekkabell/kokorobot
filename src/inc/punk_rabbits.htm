PUNK RABBITS

When Dev & I travel a lot in the summer it is hard to stay focused and to work on big illustration projects, but I wanted to keep drawing and so I decided to draw a series of rabbit characters. Most rabbits aren't especially "punk", but it was the working title for the project and I've just decided to keep it.

I started the series on July 1st 2025, and finished it on January 3rd 2026. Thank you to everyone who followed along with the release of each rabbit!
